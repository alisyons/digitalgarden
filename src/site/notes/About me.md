### Why make this site?

I'm growing ==increasingly annoyed== with the current (2025) state of the internet, social media and algorithms. Nowadays it's impossible to take it slow when building an online presence, and you are bombarded by ads. Also I want to stay in touch with my coding skills and this site gives me enough of a playground without me having to build a site from scratch.

### What did you use to build this?

This site is made in Obsidian and published via the [Digital Garden Plugin](https://dg-docs.ole.dev/). I'm also using the [Ukiyo Theme](https://github.com/technerium/obsidian-ukiyo). Some site views (such as the topics pages) have data views coded by me using the [dataviewjs](https://blacksmithgu.github.io/obsidian-dataview/) framework. I have also incorporated some of my custom stylings on e.g. the tag color.
